The Ultimate PDF Sharing Checklist  
Sharing PDFs can be a breeze with Adobe Acrobat. Whether you're 
collaborating on a project, seeking feedback, or simply distributing 
information, this checklist will ensure you cover all the bases. Let's dive 
into the essentials of sharing PDFs effectively.  
General Sharing Checklist  
•  Open Your PDF  
• Have you launched Acrobat or Acrobat Reader and opened 
the PDF you want to share?  
•  Choose Your Sharing Method  
• Are you sharing via email, link, or a third -party app like 
Micros oft Teams or WhatsApp?  
•  Set Permissions  
• Have you decided if recipients can comment or just view the 
document?  
•  Add Recipients  
• Did you enter the email addresses of everyone who needs 
access?  
•  Customize Your Message  
• Have you added a personal touch to the sub ject and message 
fields?  
•  Create and Share the Link  
• Did you generate a shareable link and copy it for distribution?
